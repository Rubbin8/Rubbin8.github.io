• This is a javascript game that I made in highschool called "Neon Tunnel." It was a passion project of mine for a while, but I ended up abandoning it when I switched to learning C++.

  • "Neon Tunnel" is about navigating through hyperspace in a super fast spaceship. The player needs to avoid obstacles, either by swerving out of the way or by shooting them.

  • You can play the game by using either the arrow keys or A and D to steer left and right, and by using either the down key or S to shoot.
