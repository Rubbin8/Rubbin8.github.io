The world of our DnD campagin

I'm a DM, that's short for "Dungeon Master," which means that I'm in charge of
creating the world for the other DnD players to play in as their characters.

The world I created for our campagn at Redbird Gaming Club is mostly covered
in a massive ocean, with one giant continent. This continent has a ring of grasslands
and forests along the coastlines, while the middle is covered in a giant desert that
appears to boil, as the sand constantly moves, making it impossible to stand on.

Giant wooden spires tower above this desert, cylindrical cities that stretch over
a mile into the air, each appearing to be made out of one singular peice of wood.
These spires are connected by thick, incredibly long cables that carry cable cars
between the spires. These cable cars, or gondalas, are the size of buildings, and
many of them are small businesses, with others being owned by the government of
the continent, known as the nation of Tonthrop.
