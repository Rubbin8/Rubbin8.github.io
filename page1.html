Redbird Gaming Club

The Redbird Gaming club is a student club at Skagit Valley College that I run with my friend Ace.
We play Dungeons and Dragons every other week, and on the weeks in between we play other games such as Catan,
Jackbox, various card games, and more!

We meet every Friday at 4PM in the Birch office 100 at 4 pm, and
play games until around 7 or 8, depending on how it goes.
